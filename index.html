About RAWA2 Media

RAWA2 Media, founded by Aseel and Issa, offers content around mental health, relationships, and self-improvement from both male and female perspectives. As mental health advocates, we aim to spark change and create a safe space for discussions. Our podcast delves into social situations, self-growth, and emotional well-being.

Our Mission

RAWA2 Media strives to make social interactions more peaceful by fostering understanding and empathy. Through podcasts, articles, and other projects, we aim to create positive change and help individuals connect in healthier, more supportive ways.
